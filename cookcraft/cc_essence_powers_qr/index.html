    CookCraft Essence Quick Reference¶

    Streamlined tag effects for easy lookup

    How to Use This Reference¶

    Duration by Category:

    • Sustenance: 1 hour × tag level (max 24h) – Environmental protection
    • Enhancement: 30 min × tag level (max 10h) – Combat/skill boosts
    • Utility: 30 min × tag level (max 10h) – Special capabilities

    Symbols:

    • @ = Needs review/refinement
    • ~ = No application for this category

    COMMON PATTERNS¶

    Agent Creation (Venom/Paralyze/Disease/Confusion):

    • 1 minute to create
    • One object at a time (new replaces old)
    • DC = 10 + your level
    • Lasts until triggered or duration ends
    • Cannot treat food/drinks/worn items

    Touch Abilities (Healing/Drain):

    • 1 action, touch range
    • Cooldown prevents spam (10-30 min)
    • Strategic timing required

    Sustained Effects (Charm/Dark/Sense):

    • Require setup time (1-10 min)
    • Need continuous effort or conditions
    • End if conditions break

    QUICK LOOKUP: COMMON NEEDS¶

    Need Resistance? Fire, Cold, Acid, Lightning, Sonic, Force, Mental (Psychic), Poison (Venom), Physical (Earth)

    Need Movement? Flight (fly 20 ft), Swim (swim speed), Burrow (15 ft), Mobility (+10 all speeds)

    Need Social Bonuses? Charm (+2 Diplomacy/Deception), Sonic (+2 Performance/Intimidation), Light (+2 in bright areas)

    Need Combat Bonuses? Sharp (+1 attacks), Defense (+1 AC), Fire (+2 Reflex), Lightning (+1 Initiative)

    Need Detection? Vision (see invisibility), Scent (track 30 ft), Sense (sentinel awareness), Magical (detect magic)

    Need Healing? Healing (2d8 touch), Regeneration (remove wounded), Drain (vampiric)
